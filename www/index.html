⚡ How to Play

Watch the pads light up in sequence. Then repeat the sequence from memory by clicking or tapping the pads.

Each round adds one more step to the sequence. The playback speeds up as you progress. At level 8, two extra pads unlock!

Daily Challenge: Same sequence for everyone. 1 life. Share your score!

Endless: 3 lives. How far can you go?

Keyboard: Press 1-6 for pads. Esc to quit.
